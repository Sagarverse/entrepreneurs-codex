# The Entrepreneur's Codex — Play Store / App Store copy

## Short description (≤80 chars, Play Store)
A 30-day journey of mindset, skill, and action — forged in ink and gold.

## Long description (≤4000 chars, Play Store)
The Entrepreneur's Codex is a cinematic 30-day training for the
entrepreneurial mind. Each day opens a new chapter — part essay,
part trial, part reflection — composed in a hand-set serif
typeface over a deep ink-and-gold field. There are no streaks to
chase. There is only the next page.

WHAT YOU GET
• 30 chapters across 4 weeks — abundance, craft, action, and
  monetization
• A daily manifesto, three trials, a curated video, a reflection
  prompt, and a five-question examination
• A living seal that fills as you progress
• A printable PDF Certificate of Completion when you seal the
  final chapter
• A journal of your reflections, kept private on your device

DESIGNED TO BE RETURNED TO
Every chapter ends with a teaser for the next, so tomorrow's page
already exists in your mind by the time you close the app. Sound
is minimal — a single Arabic bell — so the experience is felt, not
performed.

PRIVATE BY DEFAULT
Your reflections, your XP, your progress: all stored on your
device. No accounts. No servers. No tracking. The auto-update
check contacts GitHub's public Releases API once at launch so you
always have the latest chapter; nothing about you leaves the app.
